Frequently Asked Questions

In case you have more questions, feel free to reach out to us.






What is VCarro App?

VCarro is a tech-enabled logistics company offering a variety of intracity and intercity delivery services. Just download and register yourself on the app, choose the service that best fits yours logistic needs and make your booking! ith VCarro, you will get a verified driver and vehicle right at your doorstep.

How do I use VCarro App?

Follow these simple steps to start leveraging hassle-free logistics from VCarro: 1.Download the VCarro app 2.Choose the service you want to use 3.Select your pick up and delivery locations 4.Add any extra stops, if applicable 5.Choose the most appropriate vehicle type as per your requirements (2wheeler, three-wheelers or trucks) 6.Select the type of goods you’re sending 7.Choose your payment method 8.Click on “Book Now” and your vehicle is on its way!

Does VCarro provide intercity or interstate service?

VCarro provides intracity & intercity services. For instant goods delivery within the city, you can choose VCarro 2-wheelers and trucks. We also have intercity outstation services within a 300km radius of the city center. You can also send goods across India with VCarro intercity courier services at economical rates. VCarro also provides intercity and intracity house shifting services

What are the charges for a VCarro truck and VCarro bike delivery service?

The charges for hiring a VCarro truck or bike are based on the number of kilometres travelled and the type of vehicle chosen and this differs from locality to locality. You can also check the estimate tab on the VCarro website to get an exact idea of your final trip amount. Alternatively, the final trip amount can be determined when making the booking on the app.

What does VCarro deliver?

From a pin to an entire house, we will deliver anything, anywhere, anytime. However, VCarro does not deliver item(s) which are illegal, dangerous or hazardous in nature or which are prohibited by any statue or law.

How long does it take to deliver?

In the case of intracity deliveries, the distance & traffic play a major role in determining the delivery time however the instant availability of vehicles allows you to send goods up to 2500 kgs. VCarro intercity courier services allows you to deliver goods at economical rates within a couple of days.

Does VCarro provide mini trucks like tata ace on monthly contract?

We provide on-demand availability of mini trucks like Tata Ace. For bulk orders and frequent uses, we provide VCarro for Enterprise service with one app-multi-city-mutli user access.

How does an API Integration with VCarro work?

VCarro's APIs are simple to integrate and use. The simple layouts will be easily adaptable and will start creating orders directly from your order management system.

How does an API integration solve business problems?

An API Integration with VCarro helps companies with webhook updates, live tracking, and delivery authentication across 16 cities. Utilise a standardised 2-wheeler service with zero hassles.

What vehicle categories are available via the APIs?

VCarro's APIs are currently available on 2-wheelers. API integration on other vehicles will be launched soon.
